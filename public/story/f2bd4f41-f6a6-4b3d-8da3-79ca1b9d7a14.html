Stripe Acquires Fintech Startup Lemon for $850M

Payment giant Stripe announced the acquisition of Lemon, a Y Combinator-backed fintech startup specializing in embedded banking solutions for SaaS companies. The deal values Lemon at $850 million, marking Stripe's largest acquisition since 2021. Lemon's technology enables software companies to offer banking services directly within their applications, including checking accounts, debit cards, and lending products. The startup serves over 500 SaaS companies and processes $2 billion in annual transaction volume.
