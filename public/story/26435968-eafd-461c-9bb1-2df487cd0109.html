SaaS Valuations Rebound to 8.2x Revenue Multiple

WSJ | 11/26/2025, 7:59:55 AM

Impact Score: 6.00/10

Software-as-a-Service companies saw median valuations rise to 8.2x revenue in Q4 2025, up from 6.1x a year ago, signaling renewed investor confidence in the sector. The recovery follows two years of compressed multiples after the 2022 market correction. High-growth companies with strong unit economics are commanding premium valuations, with some AI-focused SaaS companies trading above 15x revenue.
