Impact Score Legend:
  • 8-10: Very high PE relevance - Major M&A, IPO, or market-moving news
  • 6-7: Moderate-high relevance - Notable trends or sector developments
  • 4-5: Neutral - General industry news with some PE context
  • 2-3: Low relevance - Tangential to PE interests
  • 0-1: Very low relevance - Minimal PE investment implications
